## Spec Summary

Implements the Home Screen that displays a 2-column grid of muscle group (body part) cards. Each card shows a representative icon and the capitalized body part name. Tapping a card navigates to the Exercise List Screen. Includes loading, error (with retry), and empty-list states. Wires the domain layer (`ExerciseRepository` interface + `GetBodyPartsUseCase`) and registers all bindings in the Koin DI modules.
